Vamo! - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Introduction

Vamo! ("we", "our", or "us") is a travel discovery app that helps users explore cities and find places of interest. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Location Data

We collect your precise location data to show you nearby places, restaurants, and points of interest. Location data is used solely for app functionality and is not linked to your identity or used for tracking purposes.

Device Identifiers

Our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers (such as advertising IDs) to serve relevant advertisements within the app. This data may be used for tracking across apps and websites.

Usage Data

We collect product interaction data (such as app launches, taps, and feature usage) through Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and improve the experience. Advertising data is collected by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads.

Diagnostics

We collect crash data and performance data through Firebase to identify and fix bugs, improve app stability, and optimize performance. This data is not linked to your identity.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Places API — to search and display places near your location
  • Google AdMob — to display advertisements
  • Firebase — for analytics, crash reporting, and performance monitoring

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed above for the purposes described in this policy.

Data Retention

Location data is used in real-time to provide search results and is not stored on our servers. Analytics and diagnostics data is retained by our third-party providers according to their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Deny or revoke location permissions through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
